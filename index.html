Message From The Secretary General
"Leaders are supposed to create leaders better than them."

As Secretary-General of SMITMUN, I am honored to extend a warm welcome to all participants, delegates, and visitors to our Model United Nations platform. At SMITMUN, we believe in nurturing the leaders of tomorrow, empowering them to surpass the achievements of their predecessors. Model United Nations serves as an invaluable tool in this journey of leadership development. It offers a simulated platform where individuals can delve into the complexities of global issues, hone their diplomatic skills, and learn the art of negotiation and compromise. Through active participation in debates, resolutions, and diplomacy, delegates cultivate essential traits such as leadership, confidence, and boldness. The experiences gained from SMITMUN go far beyond the confines of the conference room. They extend into various facets of life, including career pursuits. The skills acquired through Model United Nations are highly sought after by employers in diverse fields, be it corporate, military, or government sectors. SMITMUN alumni have excelled in interviews for prestigious positions in the navy, marine engineering, and various roles beyond the borders of India. Our alumni stand as living testament to the transformative power of Model United Nations. They carry with them not only the knowledge gained but also the confidence and personality developed through their participation in SMITMUN. They are equipped to navigate the challenges of the real world and make meaningful contributions to society. As we continue to foster the next generation of leaders, I urge all participants to embrace this opportunity fully. Seize every moment to learn, grow, and inspire others. Together, let us strive to create leaders who will not only surpass us but also lead with compassion, integrity, and vision.

Warm regards,
Cdt. Kumar Aakarsh
Secretary-General

What is SMITMUN?

SMITMUN, short for the Sikkim Manipal Institute of Technology Model United Nations, is a premier Model United Nations conference held annually in Sikkim, North-East India. Organized by students for students, SMITMUN provides a platform for delegates to engage in simulated diplomatic negotiations, debates, and discussions on global issues. The conference aims to promote understanding of international affairs, hone delegates' skills in diplomacy, negotiation, public speaking, and research, and foster a spirit of collaboration and leadership. With a focus on innovation and excellence, SMITMUN offers a diverse range of committees and topics, ensuring that participants from various backgrounds and interests find engaging and relevant discussions. Beyond the committee sessions, SMITMUN features networking opportunities, cultural exchanges, and social events, creating a vibrant and enriching experience for all attendees. Whether you're a seasoned delegate or new to Model UN, SMITMUN welcomes you to join the conversation, explore new perspectives, and make lasting connections. Join us at SMITMUN and be part of a dynamic community dedicated to shaping a better world through dialogue and diplomacy.
