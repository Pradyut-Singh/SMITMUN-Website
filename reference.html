WHAT IS SMITMUN

SMITMUN organizes Model United Nations conferences, simulating the United Nations and its various committees. Students take on the roles of delegates, representing different countries and debating real-world issues. Through research, negotiation, and resolution writing, participants gain valuable experience in:

    1.  Public speaking and debate

    2.  Research and critical analysis

    3.  Teamwork and collaboration

    4.  Negotiation and compromise

 

WHY PARTICIPATE IN SMITMUN

SMITMUN offers a unique opportunity for students to:

    1.  Develop essential life skills

    2.  Gain a deeper understanding of international relations

    3.  Network with students from diverse backgrounds

    4.  Build confidence and leadership qualities
